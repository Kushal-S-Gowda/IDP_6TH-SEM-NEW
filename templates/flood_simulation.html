What This Demonstrates
This predictive model simulates urban flood inundation using real Bengaluru elevation data and physics-based water flow — the SCS Curve Number hydrology method adapted for cellular automaton (Central Water Commission guidelines). Soil saturation, drain capacity, and lake overflow are derived from real monsoon data.

Purpose: Physics-based inundation map — water follows elevation, drains, and user-set rainfall (mm/hr × hours). Outputs feed Early Warning via “Send Alert”. 2022 scenario uses IMD-reported ~135 mm/24h totals.
